Django Reinhardt

Guitarist virtuoso of the jazz era

  • Born January 23rd 1910
  • Gets first guitar at age 12
  • Begans making a living playing music at age 15
  • Forms Quintette du Hot Club de France in 1934
  • Becomes the best guitarist to grace the instrument in the history of guitar (facts!)
  • Dies suddenly to a stroke at age 43 May 16th 1953
